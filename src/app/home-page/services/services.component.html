Routines

Our Routines service offers personalized workout plans tailored to individual needs. Trainers can create custom routines, and clients can choose a trainer to request a specific workout plan. This personalized approach ensures that each client receives a routine that aligns with their fitness goals, preferences, and schedule.

BMI Calculator

The BMI Calculator is a user-friendly tool that simplifies the process of calculating Body Mass Index. It serves as a convenient resource for both clients and trainers, allowing them to quickly determine BMI as a measure of body fat based on an individual's height and weight. This calculation helps in assessing overall health and planning fitness strategies.
