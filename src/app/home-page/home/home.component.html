Welcome to Gym X

The platform is thoughtfully designed to simplify the administration of exercise regimens, allowing trainers to efficiently tailor routines to individual client needs, while empowering clients with the tools to track their workouts. Alongside this, it boasts an integrated Body Mass Index (BMI) calculator, providing a valuable resource for users to calculate and understand their BMI as a measure of body fat based on height and weight. This feature serves as a guide for clients to gauge their fitness levels and for trainers to offer more personalized health advice.
